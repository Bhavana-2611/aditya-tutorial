About Aditya Tutorial

Aditya Tutorial is a trusted educational institute helping students successfully complete their 10th and 12th through recognized open schooling and government-approved boards.

We provide flexible learning options for students who have discontinued their studies, including both online and offline classes with expert guidance.

Our programs ensure fast-track results, enabling students to pursue higher education, government jobs, and opportunities abroad.

We are committed to delivering quality education and guiding every student towards a successful future.

Chairman's Message

At Aditya Tutorial, our vision is to empower every student with a second opportunity to succeed in life through quality education and structured guidance. We strongly believe that failure is not the end, but a stepping stone towards greater achievements. Our institution focuses on providing flexible learning methods for students who have discontinued their studies, ensuring they can complete their education without pressure. With both online and offline support, we guide students at every stage of their academic journey. We are committed to delivering quick results, recognized certifications, and opportunities for higher education, government jobs, and international careers. Our mission is to build confidence, remove fear, and help every student move forward with determination and success.
